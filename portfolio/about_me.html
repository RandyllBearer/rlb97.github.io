About Me
The author of this blog is Randyll Bearer, who was at the time of this writing a Senior Computer Science student at the University of Pittsburgh. As part of his curriculum for his last semester before graduating, Randyll took the course ENGCMP0610: Composing Digital Media instructed by Professor Matthew Lavin. The course was centered around learning how to compose a range of critical media objects using HTML, Audacity, Photoshop, and learning how to apply design principles to those projects. This blog will act as the online portfolio repository for that class, a showcase of Randyll's digital media creations. The author's perpeptual goal for this blog is to complete and post a summary of each in-class assignment and/or project as they are completed. Hopefully this blog will provide some insight into the author's motivations and/or ideas while creating these projects.
