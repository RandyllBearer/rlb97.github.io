1. An example of how you made a choice related to genre or audience.

For this first section of my Final Portfolio, I wanted to present my adverts created for Week 6 and revised for Work in Progress 2. Below is the original advert:

And the following is the result of my first revision I did in class:

As you can see, I primarily only initally made one change in moving the graphic to center stage. I did this because I feel that most advertisements these days do not care about the technical specifics as much as they do presenting the user/potential customer with a display of pathos. No contemporary car commercials are describing turn radius or torque, rather they are trying to show how cool one is when driving the car. With this edit, I wanted to emphasize how happy/content the customer will be with their purchase.

The above is my second revision of the advert. In it, I wanted to rebrand the slogan in order to evoke a nostalgic/retro feel and vibe as well as the contemporary era seems to be one composed of nostalgia. Graphics hardware is in heavy demand at the moment due to its usefulness in crypto-mining, so I wanted to appeal to that audience and potential customer base as well.
